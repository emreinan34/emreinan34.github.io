Account and Data Deletion

You have the right to request the permanent deletion of your LinguaLeap account. This action is irreversible. Please choose one of the two methods below to proceed.

Option 1: Delete From Inside the App

This is the fastest and recommended method if you have access to the application.

  1. Open the LinguaLeap app on your device and log in.
  2. Navigate to the Settings tab using the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap the "Delete Account" button.
  4. Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm the permanent deletion of your account.

Option 2: Submit a Web Request

If you no longer have access to the app, you can submit a deletion request using our web form. Please ensure you provide the email address associated with your LinguaLeap account. Requests are processed manually and may take a few business days to complete.

Data Deletion Information

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon confirming your account deletion request, the following data associated with your account will be permanently removed from our live systems:

  • Account Credentials: Your authentication information, including your email address and any stored login credentials from Firebase Authentication.
  • Personal Information: Your chosen Display Name / Username.
  • Quiz and Score Data: All of your high scores, session history, and progress data for all language pairs and quiz durations.
  • Leaderboard Entries: Your username and scores will be removed from all public leaderboards.

Data That May Be Retained

To maintain and improve our services, some data is retained in an aggregated and fully anonymized form. This data cannot be linked back to you personally after your account has been deleted. This includes:

  • Aggregated Analytics: Anonymized app usage data (e.g., feature popularity statistics).
  • Anonymized Performance Data: Anonymized crash logs and performance metrics.

Data Retention in Backups

While your data is deleted from our live production systems immediately, it may persist in secure, encrypted backup archives for up to 90 days before being permanently erased.
