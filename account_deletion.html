Account Deletion Instructions

You can request the deletion of your LinguaLeap account and all associated data directly from within the app by following these steps:

1. Navigate to the Settings tab within the app.
2. Scroll to the bottom and tap the "Delete Account" button.
3. A confirmation dialog will appear. To proceed, tap the "Delete" button.

This action is irreversible and will permanently delete all your scores and profile information.
