Privacy Policy for LinguaLeap

 

Effective Date: July 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Emre Ä°nan ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of our mobile application, LinguaLeap (the "App").  

 

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services, including:  

 

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Email Address & Authentication Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication details (such as a hashed password or authentication tokens from Google Sign-In) to create and secure your account.

  • Display Name / Username: We collect the username you choose, which is used to identify you on public leaderboards within the App.  

 

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Quiz Performance Data: We store your quiz scores and progress for different language pairs and durations to provide you with your high score reports and to rank you on leaderboards.

  • Usage and Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics for Firebase to collect data about how you interact with the App. This includes information like which screens you visit, which buttons you tap, session duration, and general device information (e.g., device model, OS version). This helps us understand what features are popular and how to improve the user experience.

  • Crash and Performance Data: We use Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect crash reports and performance data. This helps us identify and fix bugs, and to monitor the stability of the App.  

 

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the App's core functionality (quizzes, scoring, etc.).

  • To create and manage your user account.

  • To display your username and score on public leaderboards.

  • To personalize your experience.

  • To analyze usage and trends to improve the App.

  • To diagnose and fix technical issues and crashes. 

 

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect information as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services: Used for core application services on Android.

  • Firebase (a Google service): We use multiple Firebase services including Authentication, Firestore (database), Cloud Functions, Crashlytics, and Analytics. [Link to Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

  • Google AdMob: We use AdMob to display advertisements within the App. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads. You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites or apps that use their services here: [How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

 

4. Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and its associated data at any time. You can initiate this process from the "Delete Account" option within the App's Settings screen.

When you request account deletion, the following data is permanently removed from our live systems:

  • Account Credentials: Your authentication information, including your email address and any stored login credentials, are deleted from Firebase Authentication.

  • Personal Information: Your chosen Display Name / Username.

  • Quiz and Score Data: All of your high scores, session history, and progress data for all language pairs and quiz durations stored in our Firestore database are deleted.

  • Leaderboard Entries: Your username and scores are removed from all leaderboards.

 

Data We May Retain

Certain data may be retained in an aggregated and anonymized form after your account has been deleted. This data cannot be linked back to you personally. This includes:

  • Aggregated Analytics: Anonymized app usage and interaction data (e.g., which features are used most often) collected by Google Analytics for Firebase is retained for statistical analysis and service improvement.

  • Anonymized Performance Data: Anonymized crash logs and performance metrics from Firebase Crashlytics are retained to ensure the stability of the App. 

 

Data Retention Period

Upon your confirmation, the deletion of your data from our live production systems is initiated immediately. Due to the nature of our secure backup systems, your data may persist in encrypted backup archives for up to 90 days before being permanently and irretrievably erased.

 

5. Children's Privacy

LinguaLeap is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. 

 

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
