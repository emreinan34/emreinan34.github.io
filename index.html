Account Deletion Instructions

LinguaLeap Account and Data Deletion This page provides instructions on how to permanently delete your LinguaLeap account and provides information on what data is removed. How to Request Account Deletion You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time from within the LinguaLeap application. Open the LinguaLeap app on your device and log in. Navigate to the "Settings" screen using the bottom navigation bar. Tap on the "Delete Account" button. Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm the permanent deletion of your account. Please note: This action is final and cannot be undone. Data That Will Be Deleted Upon confirming your account deletion request, the following data associated with your account will be permanently removed from our live systems: Account Credentials: Your authentication information, including your email address and any stored login credentials from Firebase Authentication. Personal Information: Your chosen Display Name / Username. Quiz and Score Data: All of your high scores, session history, and progress data for all language pairs and quiz durations stored in our Firestore database. Leaderboard Entries: Your username and scores will be removed from all public leaderboards. Data That Is Retained To maintain and improve our services, some data is retained in an aggregated and fully anonymized form. This data cannot be linked back to you personally after your account has been deleted. This includes: Aggregated Analytics: Anonymized app usage data (e.g., feature popularity statistics) collected by Google Analytics for Firebase. Anonymized Performance Data: Anonymized crash logs and performance metrics from Firebase Crashlytics used for stability monitoring. Data Retention Period in Backups While your data is deleted from our live databases immediately upon request, it may persist in secure, encrypted backup archives for up to 90 days. After this period, the data is permanently erased from all backup systems. Contact Us If you have any questions about the account deletion process, please contact us at: lingualeap.app@gmail.com
